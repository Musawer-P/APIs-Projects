Mission Details

Falcon 9 Flight 20 (Orbcomm-OG2)

#Flight - 20

2015/22/12

Cape Canaveral, SLC-40

Falcon 9

The Falcon 9 Flight 20 mission successfully launched 11 Orbcomm-OG2 satellites into orbit. This mission was especially historic because for the first time, the rocket’s first stage performed a successful vertical landing back on Earth — marking a major milestone in reusable rocket technology.

Falcon Heavy Test Flight

#Flight - 1

2018/06/02

Kennedy Space Center, LC-39A

Falcon Heavy

The Falcon Heavy Test Flight carried Elon Musk’s Tesla Roadster into orbit. It demonstrated the rocket’s immense lifting capability, with two side boosters successfully landing simultaneously back on Earth.

Crew Dragon Demo-2

#Flight - 85

2020/30/05

Kennedy Space Center, LC-39A

Falcon 9

Crew Dragon Demo-2 was SpaceX’s first crewed mission, sending NASA astronauts Doug Hurley and Bob Behnken to the International Space Station, marking the return of human spaceflight from U.S. soil after nearly a decade.

Starship Flight Test 3

#Flight - 3

2024/14/03

Starbase, Texas

Starship

The third integrated flight test of Starship reached space and reentered Earth’s atmosphere for the first time, showcasing major progress toward a fully reusable spacecraft capable of interplanetary travel.
