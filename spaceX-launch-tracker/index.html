Falcon 9 Flight 20 (Orbcomm-OG2)

#Flight - 20

2015/22/12

Cape Canaveral, SLC-40

Falcon 9

The Falcon 9 Flight 20 mission successfully launched 11 Orbcomm-OG2 satellites into orbit. This mission was especially historic because for the first time, the rocket’s first stage performed a successful vertical landing back on Earth — marking a major milestone in reusable rocket technology.
