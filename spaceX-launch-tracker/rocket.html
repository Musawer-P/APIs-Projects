Rockets

Falcon 1

Small, two-stage, LOX/RP-1 rocket. Designed for small payloads to low Earth orbit.

Falcon 9

Medium-lift, two-stage LOX/RP-1 rocket. Reusable first stage. Used for satellite launches, ISS resupply, crewed missions.

Falcon Heavy

Heavy-lift rocket: basically three Falcon 9 first stages strapped together (side boosters + center core), second stage same or similar to Falcon 9’s second stage.

Dragon / Cargo Dragon / Crew Dragon

Spacecraft, not a rocket. The Dragon series includes cargo versions (Dragon 1) and crewed versions (Dragon 2, “Crew Dragon”).

Starship / Super Heavy

The next generation: fully reusable heavy-lift system. Super Heavy = booster, Starship = second stage / spacecraft. Intended for deep space missions, Moon, Mars, large payloads.
