Atomic Habits

Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a powerful and practical guide to building good habits, breaking bad ones, and mastering the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results. The book emphasizes that real change comes from the compound effect of hundreds of small decisions — doing two push-ups a day, waking up five minutes earlier, or holding a single short phone call.
